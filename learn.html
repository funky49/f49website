funky49 is Steven Rush, a rapper/producer/dj and science enthusiast living in Tampa, FL, USA, Earth. He has rapped for both Chuck D and Bill Nye.

funky49 has performed at geek-friendly events such as Orlando Nerd Fest (2014-2015), Nerdapalooza (2007-2012), the St. Pete Science Festival (2012-present), Tampa Bay Comic Con (2016-???) and the USA Science and Engineering Festival (2012). He is most likely the only rapper to have performed at the Smithsonian National Air & Space Museum and the National Science Foundation. He’s been on the same bill as most major nerdcore superstars and once flew to Eugene, Oregon to open for mc chris. He was named a “Nerdcore Hip-Hop All Star” by Wired magazine. His science outreach via hip-hop music video for Fermilab has over 60,000 views.

funky49 has also given presentations at the National Academy of Sciences, the Fermi National Accelerator Laboratory, BarCamp Tampa and was honored to tryout for TED. Not just a solo performer, he is a proud member of the Orlando nerdcore supergroup, Emergency Pizza Party.

funky49 has been lucky enough to attend #NASASocial events at the Kennedy Space Center (Mars Curiosity launch, Mars MAVEN launch). Space Camp USA invited him to a Tweetup where they made him COMMANDER of the ENTERPRISE.
